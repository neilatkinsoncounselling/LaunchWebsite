Session formats

How I work

Counselling should fit around your life, not the other way around. Choose the format that feels right, or ask and I can help you decide.

  • Face-to-Face Counselling

    "I just need someone to talk to."

    A private, one-to-one space that belongs entirely to you. In person in Consett, County Durham.

    Find out more

  • Online Counselling

    "Leaving the house feels impossible right now."

    Everything you would get in person, from wherever feels safe. Secure video or phone sessions.

    Find out more

  • Walk and Talk

    "I think more clearly when I'm moving."

    Counselling outdoors, side by side, in nature. Sometimes easier to open up when you're not sitting face to face.

    Find out more

  • Telephone

    "I'd rather not be on camera."

    No video, no screen — just a conversation. Private, accessible, and just as effective.

    Find out more

Integrative counselling with Neil Atkinson, MNCPS (Acc.). Face to face, online, telephone and walk and talk across the North East of England.
