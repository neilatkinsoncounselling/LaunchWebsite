Meet Neil

Neil Atkinson

Integrative Counsellor · MNCPS (Acc.) · NCPS Accredited Registrant

I came to counselling through real life rather than a textbook. My approach is warm, thoughtful and grounded. I work with you rather than applying a fixed method.

My aim is to offer a space where you can talk openly, feel understood and begin to make sense of things in your own way. I work with adults aged 18 and over.

What I can help with

People come to counselling for many different reasons. You might recognise something here, or you might not. Both are completely fine.
